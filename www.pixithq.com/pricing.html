Frequently asked questions

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept annual payment via credit card, check or ACH. Monthly subscriptions must be paid via credit card.

What if we have questions after we sign up?

Our support page is a great resource to answer our most frequently asked questions (link). If you require additional assistance, please reach out to your account representative to discuss any other issues or questions.

Do the payments have to be in USD?

Yes, all prices displayed are in USD.

What are the differences between each plan?

If you have additional questions that aren’t covered here, please contact a sales representative.

Do I have to sign up for a full year or can I pay monthly?

We offer both monthly and annual subscription options.

Maintenance: Are there additional fees for adding users?

Our maintenance software pricing is based per user. Adding additional admin users to your account will result in additional charges per user.

Can I start with one license and add others later?

Yes, contact your account representative when you are ready to add additional licenses and they can discuss pricing and any discounts available.

Maintenance: Is there a limit on the number of tasks/tickets I can log and will I be charged for going over that limit?

No, you can log as many tasks/tickets as you need to, there is no limit.

I have more than one location, do you offer pricing discounts for multiple locations?

Yes, contact one of our sales reps to discuss our enterprise pricing and multi-location discounts. (add link to email sales)

Lost and Found: Are there additional fees for adding users and is there a limit?

No, there is no additional cost for adding users and there is no limit on the number of users you can provide account access to.

Do you provide training when we sign up?

Our support page contains all training and setup resources you will need. If you require additional training for you or your team, you can schedule a call with your account representative. Additional costs may be incurred if support hours are not included in your software package.

Lost and Found: Is there a limit on the number of items I can log into the system and will I be charged if I exceed the limit?

No, there is no limit on the number of items that can be added to the system. We want to encourage all clients to log every item into the system so that they have the best chance of being returned to their owners.

Do you charge for support?

Each software package has a number of support hours included in the pricing. If you exceed your hours, you will be charged for the additional support. Support includes setup and training that exceeds your included hours. Any inquiries as a result of the software’s performance will not count towards your support hours.
